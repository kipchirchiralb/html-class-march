About Eldohub Academy

Introduction

We are a tech academy in eldoret focusing on providing hands-on, industry relevant training for young people in eldoret.

Mission

To empower African youth with cutting-edge tech education, nurturing their potential to become the tech leaders of tomorrow. We strive to make high-quality software development and digital skills training accessible to all, regardless of background or circumstance.

Vission

To be the leading catalyst for technological innovation and empowerment among African youth, fostering a generation of creators, problem-solvers, and leaders who drive the continent's digital transformation.
